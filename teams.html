Real-time distributed trading and risk management

C.S. Track: Distributed Systems

Mentor: Steven Mauceri

Members: Eitan Leitner, Yisroel Newmark, Samuel Orenbakh, Matias Salem, Yoni Stern, Joseph Teitelbaum

Project Description: This project will focus on the design and implementation of a scalable trading, position, and risk management system for equity products. The project will include the delivery of front-end and back-end features for a full risk management platform, support for multiple failover modes, and distribution of tasks across backend services to scale out performance.
