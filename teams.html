Deep Learning in Computer Vision

C.S. Track: AI

Mentor: Gershom Kutliroff

Members: Eitan Traurig, Tuvya Macklin, Jason Zelmanovich, Zachary Mankowitz, Charles Lando

Advances in deep learning have revolutionized the field of computer vision and are driving innovations in many fast-growing industries, notably driverless cars, autonomous robots, and AR, to name a few. In this project, we will begin with a quick introduction to convolutional neural networks and learn to work with Tensorflow, Google’s framework for developing and training ML models. Together we will select a common problem in computer vision, such as classification, detection, semantic segmentation, or pose estimation, and an appropriate dataset. Students will learn to preprocess data, train deep neural networks, techniques to validate results, and how to run inference on the generated model.

Dynamic Deep Network Models and Applications

C.S. Track: AI

Mentor: Ramesh Natarajan

Members: Joseph Couzens, Abraham Linzer, Tani Gross, Michael Kupferstein, Michael Batushansky

This project will explore and implement Dynamic Deep Network models for Machine Learning, which involve “on the fly” training and inference as new training data becomes available. This approach is especially valuable when the training data distribution is changing over time, where the models have to be continuously updated to match the evolving new distribution. We will apply the dynamic methodology to a few practical use cases.

Real-time distributed trading and risk management

C.S. Track: Distributed Systems

Mentor: Steven Mauceri

Members: Eitan Leitner, Yisroel Newmark, Samuel Orenbakh, Matias Salem, Yoni Stern, Joseph Teitelbaum

This project will focus on the design and implementation of a scalable trading, position, and risk management system for equity products. The project will include the delivery of front-end and back-end features for a full risk management platform, support for multiple failover modes, and distribution of tasks across backend services to scale out performance.
